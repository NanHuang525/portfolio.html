About Me

Hello! My name is Nan Huang. I went to Indiana University Bloomington for my undergraduate, majoring in Arts Management and minor in Studio Arts. So I was a little nervous about switching from one path to another. But after four months internship in Linkedin China, I found myself really into this field and want to explore more. One thing that is different from my previous major is the amount of research I have to do, but I have a chance to get insight into the target audiences industry, and also learn a lot about what works and what does not work when it comes to design in general. My works consist of interface design, mobile prototyping and expeirence design. Feel free to talk with me if we have the same intrests!
